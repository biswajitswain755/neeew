About Us

We Done With Your Help

A burning devotion towards Motherland, a feeling of fraternity among all citizens, we serve the society by awakening the individual.
Anandam Foundation is a non politically and non profitable Socio-Spiritual Service Trust. Founded on 4th February 2021, on the auspicious occasion of Swami Vivekananda's birthday (according to Tithi). We work all over Bharat to develop the spiritual awakening to build a stress-free society.

Our Vision :

  • Try to establish a new era of Spirituality through Bhakti, Karma and Raj Yoga.
  • Social enrichment based on spirituality.
  • Awakening people by bringing the glorious history of Bharat and about the Indian culture and traditional methods.
  • Highlighting local Heros (Freedom fighters, Saints, intellectuals etc..).
  • Making spiritual scriptures more reachable to the people and make it easier for people to read and understand.

Our Mission :

  • Establishing centres of own or contacted at different parts of the country for Yoga, Meditation, Naturopathy, Ayurveda, other traditional healing methods, Goshala, Vedic Vidyalaya and Vanaprasth Ashram.
  • Conducting confrence, seminars, different media campaigns to spread awareness of social and local issues, highlighting local eminent personalities (past & present) and fulfilling our other objectives
  • Develop the concept of “Gita-Gou-Gram” with Adapting Villages where every household would have Gita and Cow at their home and cultivate with the organic method.
  • Making Yoga and Meditation adaptive for new generation by making it attractive and different from usual like "Ananda Yoga' and “Ekant Yoga”.
  • Arranging ‘Bhakti Sangams’ (spiritual concerts).
  • Promoting Tribal culture and find out ways of protecting them.
  • Act as a bridge between elderly persons and new generation.
  • Conducting varios Camps and trainings like Yoga certificate course, Spiritual Camp, Leadership development camps, personality development camps and others.
  • Awaking the youths through various programs.
